About

Hello, I’m Leonel Olímpio Silima

PhD Researcher · MLOps / LLMOps · AI Systems

I am a computer engineer specialized in MLOps and LLMOps, with experience in both enterprise and research environments. I hold a Master’s degree in Applied Informatics and I am currently a PhD Researcher in Electrical and Computer Engineering at FEUP (University of Porto).

My work focuses on operationalizing Machine Learning and Large Language Models: CI/CD for ML, reproducible pipelines, cloud-native deployments (Docker/Kubernetes), monitoring and continuous evaluation, and building robust AI system architectures (including LLM tooling and RAG patterns).

I also have a strong background in DevOps, cloud platforms, system/network administration, and cybersecurity practices, enabling end-to-end delivery from data and models to production systems.
